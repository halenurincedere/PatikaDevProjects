My Chess Journey

A Chessboard, A Beginning

My journey with chess began when I was a child, sitting in front of a simple board with no idea that it would shape my future. What started as a game quickly became a language I loved — a way to think, focus, and grow.

  • Started playing at age 7
  • Learned from experience, not just books
  • Found peace and discipline in strategy

Teaching the Next Generation

Coaching young players is the heart of what I do. In one of our recent tournaments, 14 of my students stood proudly on stage with medals around their necks. Moments like that remind me: we’re not just teaching moves, we’re shaping minds.

  1. Over 100 students coached between ages 4–12
  2. Focus on confidence, concentration, and joy
  3. We celebrate effort, not just results

Champions at the Ankara Club Tournament

The Ankara Club Tournament was one of the most unforgettable experiences for our team. We competed under the Turkish Chess Federation and returned home with trophies — not just one, but with three separate teams! It was a celebration of effort, teamwork, and growth.

  • 3 teams, 3 trophies — one unforgettable tournament
  • Strong team spirit and sportsmanship
  • Supportive families, dedicated players, and proud coaches
